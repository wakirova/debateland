Our hack — Attention + Narrative Control

What Claude does well Developer content → YouTube amplification → comparison discourse → organic spikes. Repeat.
What Claude does not do Manage the narrative after a spike. The trust_integrity theme has 12 rows — the rarest in the dataset. That gap is exploitable.
Our edge Replicate the growth mechanics. Add a narrative risk layer Claude has no infrastructure for. Win on trust budget.
STEP 01
Detect
Monitor daily volume z-score. Spike threshold: robust-z ≥ 2.0. 13 such days in 2 years — each actionable.
›
STEP 02
Classify
Tag by narrative theme. Score: sentiment polarity × engagement. HIGH = negative + viral. Medium = neutral. Low = positive.
›
STEP 03
Respond
HIGH: unified founder message + value drop (expand limits). MEDIUM: seed developer content. LOW: amplify & cross-post.
›
STEP 04
Measure
Track sentiment delta 7 days post-event. Target: return to baseline within 5 days vs Claude's 10-day recovery average.

06

Applied case — what Higgsfield should have done

What went wrong
No trust budget, ragebait attention strategy

Ragebait earned attention without trust equity. When scandal hit, no unified founder voice, split positioning between co-founders, and no crisis response playbook. In KZ market where cancel culture is fast-moving, this is structural fragility. No Starbucks-level brand resilience to fall back on.

No personal brand Split messaging No crisis SOP
Correct sequence (data-backed)
Narrative control in 72 hours

Hour 0–6: Single unified founder post. Acknowledge, don't deflect.
Hour 6–24: Value drop — increase rate limits, announce free tier expansion. Redirect attention to user benefit.
Day 2–7: Developer content seeding. Shift narrative to workflow_agent theme (highest engagement in dataset).
Long-term: Media-train team. Align policy messaging before next launch.

Unified voice Value compensation Narrative pivot

How we know it is working

North star
Sentiment recovery time

Days to return to baseline polarity after a spike event. Target: ≤5 days. Claude's data shows 8–12 day recovery on controversy spikes.

Growth signal
Developer content share

% of weekly discourse in developer_tooling or workflow_agent themes. Target: 40%+ of tagged volume. These two themes generate 4.3× the engagement of trust content.

Competitive
Comparison mention rate

Organic "Nova vs Claude" posts per week without paid amplification. Claude's 146 comparison-tagged rows are almost entirely community-created — a benchmark to beat.
